## 🎬 IMDb Movies Analysis Project (PowerBI Dashboard) 

In this project me and my team analyzed a dataset of 999 movies sourced from Kaggle and created a PowerBI dashboard as part of the Big Blue Data Academy Data Science Bootcamp. 

🎯 Objective: Creating an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze IMDb movie performance based on genres, directors, release years, gross, age ratings as well as comparing audience ratings with critics’ scores.

💡 Key columns in this dataset include:
* Series_Title: The official title of the movie as listed on IMDb.
* Genre: The category or categories that describe the movie’s content and style (e.g., Drama, Action, Comedy). A movie may belong to multiple genres.
* IMDb_Rating: The average rating given by IMDb users, based on user votes, on a scale from 1 to 10.
* Meta_score: A weighted average score compiled by Metacritic, based on reviews from professional critics, on a scale from 0 to 100.
* Director: The individual responsible for directing the movie.
* Released_Year: The year in which the movie was officially released.
* Certificate: The age rating or content certification indicating the suitability of the movie for different audiences (e.g., G, PG-13, R).
* Gross: The total box office revenue generated by the movie, typically expressed in U.S. dollars.
